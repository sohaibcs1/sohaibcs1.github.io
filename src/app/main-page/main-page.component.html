Muhammad Sohaib

I completed my Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Nevada, Reno , under the supervision of Prof. Bahram Parvin .

My research focuses on developing machine learning methods for analyzing complex biomedical data and enabling automated interpretation. I primarily work with 2D histology and 3D organoid imaging, designing deep learning models and scalable AI systems that make these methods practical and accessible. While my work is centered on biomedical imaging, the approaches I develop are broadly applicable to machine learning and computer vision.
